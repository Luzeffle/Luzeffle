THE BRIEF & MISSION

          

            Birdie is a visual Fresh Air Monitor designed to improve indoor climates. In this visual redesign, I
            spearheaded a brand identity that merges high-tech environmental monitoring with decorative Danish product
            design. Utilizing custom bird-behavior motion typography and a vibrant dandelion yellow accent, I
            established a warm and highly interactive digital experience.
          

        

        

          
CREATIVE SYNTHESIS

          

            By analyzing product physical blueprints and user interaction paradigms, we structured an algorithmic
            interface model. The software's canvas elements act in direct harmony with user breathing patterns,
            responding with smooth micro-animations. It highlights how user interface and computer science theory merge
            seamlessly in human-centered product development.
